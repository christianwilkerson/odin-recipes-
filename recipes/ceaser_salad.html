Caesar Salad

Description

The caesar salad is coated with caesar dressing, pepper, pink himalayan salt. Also, crotons to provide a healthy crunch!

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Caesar Dressing
  • Pink Himalayan Salt & Pepper
  • Crotons
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Wash off your lettuce of choice with a proper cleaning solution.
  2. Apply caesar dressing to the lettuce followed by pink himalayan salt, pepper, and crotons.
  3. Enjoy your delicious meal!
